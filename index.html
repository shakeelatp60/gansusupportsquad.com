Your generosity has the potential to provide essential resources such as food, clean water, education, and healthcare to those who desperately need it. It can offer hope to families struggling to make ends meet, children yearning for an education, and communities striving to break free from the cycle of poverty.

a world about us

With a single donation, we can make a significant impact by providing financial support to low-income individuals in Gansu, China, specifically to help them afford their daily meals. By allocating the funds towards lunch money for those in need, we can ensure that vulnerable individuals and families have access to nutritious meals, which is essential for their well-being and overall health. This donation can alleviate the financial burden on low-income individuals in Gansu and contribute to improving their quality of life, fostering a stronger and more resilient community.

this donation can foster a sense of dignity and inclusivity within the community. By ensuring that everyone has access to a meal, regardless of their financial situation, we promote social equality and reduce the stigma associated with poverty. It sends a powerful message that everyone deserves to be nourished and cared for, irrespective of their socioeconomic status.

Our mission

In the heart of the International School of Beijing (ISB), a group of remarkable kids has come together with a shared mission: to bring hope and support to underprivileged communities in Gansu, China. These compassionate young individuals are dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of those less fortunate, and they are seeking your generous support and donations to help them achieve their goals.
